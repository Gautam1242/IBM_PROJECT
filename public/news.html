News and Highlights

FoodFlow Receives Prestigious Community Service Award

Date: July 1, 2024

We are honored to announce that FoodFlow has been awarded the 2024 Community Service Award for our outstanding efforts in combating hunger and food waste. This recognition is a testament to the hard work and dedication of our volunteers, donors, and partners. Together, we are making a significant impact in our communities.

New Partnership with Local Farmers

Date: June 15, 2024

FoodFlow is excited to partner with local farmers to provide fresh produce to those in need. This initiative aims to ensure that everyone has access to nutritious, locally-sourced food while supporting our local agricultural community. Together, we can reduce food waste and promote sustainable practices.

Volunteer Spotlight: Meet Sarah Johnson

Date: May 20, 2024

Sarah Johnson has been a dedicated volunteer with FoodFlow for over two years. Her commitment to helping distribute meals and organize food drives has made a tremendous difference in our efforts to fight hunger. Sarah's passion and dedication inspire us all to do more. Thank you, Sarah!
